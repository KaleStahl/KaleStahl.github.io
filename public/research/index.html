Research

I am currently supported with funding as an NSF Graduate Research Fellow.

In general, I am interested in PDE and their applications to physics. I don’t have an official advisor at the moment, but I have been working on some introductory problems with Dr. Isaac Harric the past few semesters. At the moment I have been working on projects with numerical solutions to the Biharmonic scattering problem with approximate far-field data using a MUSIC algorithm and the Born approximation.

At K-State I worked with Dr. Dinh-Liem Nguyen and Dr. Trung Truong on projects involving shape reconstruction in acoustic inverse scattering problems. I have worked previously with Dr. Yulia Hristova and Dr. Aditya Viswanathan in the 2022 University of Michigan-Dearborn REU For Analysis and Application on a project detecting edge signals from phaseless signal measurements.
